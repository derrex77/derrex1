About him

He is a Passionate Afrobeat

Victor Onyekachi Nwobie Is an Afrobeat Singer, Songwriter, Producer. He is from Ogba/Egbema/Ndoni Local Government Area In Rivers State Nigeria. He started his musical journey when he was 8 years old. A self taught Singer who is a Passionate contributor towards Music, Who is Pursuing Knowledge to excel in future endeavor and helping people to enhance there Musical talents with practical experience and Knowledge. He is the Founder/Owner of Rex Nation And referred to as Presient of Rex Nation and an Artist. He is also a Youtuber where He shares his Music with everyone. He has also done many contributions in various music with his colleagues.
